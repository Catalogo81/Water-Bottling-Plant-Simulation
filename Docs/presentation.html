Research Objectives

Objective 1

Develop a digital twin model that simulates a smart manufacturing environment through a case study, to establish baseline downtime measurements

Objective 2

Investigate and implement AI techniques (RL, GA, PSO) within the digital twin framework for reducing system downtime

Objective 3

Develop and evaluate an ensemble AI model that coordinates multiple AI techniques for optimal downtime reduction

Objective 4

Validate the ensemble AI model through comprehensive testing scenarios and optimize based on performance results

Four-Phase Methodology

Phase 1: Digital Twin Development

Build high-fidelity simulation of CUT bottling plant, establish baseline downtime metrics and validate accuracy

Phase 2: Individual AI Implementation

Develop RL, GA, and PSO solutions independently, measure individual performance and create benchmarks

Phase 3: Ensemble Integration

Create coordination framework, implement ensemble decision engine, and integrate systems

Phase 4: Validation & Optimization

Test across multiple scenarios, optimize based on downtime reduction results, and perform statistical analysis
